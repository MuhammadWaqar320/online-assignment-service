If you would like to know about ordering from Thehomeworkwriters.com in more detail, please take a moment to go through the list below:

  1. Define the type of assignment, its deadline and the number of pages or words (counting 250 words per page).
  2. Check the price quote for your order on the price calculator. If you are not sure if you have chosen the right type, please click on request a custom quote link to send us an inquiry for a customized price quote.
  3. Next step of the ordering procedure is clicking on "Order now” link, or visiting our order page directly, to proceed to the order page.
  4. Fill out all the fields of the order form. Please read the help box on the order page if not sure.
  5. Select your preferred payment method, check the “agree to terms” checkbox and proceed to the payment page.
  6. After the payment is processed, you will be taken to an order confirmation page with your order details. This is an important part of the ordering procedure. Please note that we will send you a confirmation email with instructions on how to use your control panel.
  7. Control panel: once you have your order number on hand, you will be able to login to your personal control panel, where you can check the progress of your order live, upload additional files, communicate with the writer directly, download drafts, request revisions, if needed, and leave your feedback about the writer/our service.

We hope you enjoy our services and join the community of thousands of our customers!

Please note: If you have questions about the above steps, or if there is still something unclear about the ordering procedure, please feel free to contact Customer Support via Live Chat, email us at support@thehomeworkwriters.com
